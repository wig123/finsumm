Core Conclusion: The automotive chip revenue landscape has shifted from single leadership to a dual-power structure, with NVDA relatively lagging.

Business Implications:

- Mobileye and QCOM have similar scale (volume) in the automotive business.
- NVDA's relative contribution in this specific segment is limited.

Risk Focus:
