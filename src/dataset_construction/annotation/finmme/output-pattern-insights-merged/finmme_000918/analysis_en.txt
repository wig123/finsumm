Core Conclusion: Market holdings lean towards shorter duration, while official holdings lean towards longer duration.

Business Implications:
- Overall duration is lengthened by official positioning, reducing market sensitivity.
- Interest rate changes in the market primarily impact medium-to-short-term holdings.
- Official duration is concentrated in the long end, which helps stabilize long-term interest rate levels.

Risk Concerns:
