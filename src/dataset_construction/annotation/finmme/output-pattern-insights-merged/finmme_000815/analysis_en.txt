[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The Gross Profit Margin structure of products and services shifted from "Product significantly dominant" to "Gap narrows, then widens again."

Business Implications:
- The overall profitability structure shifts from a single high-profit source to a more balanced structure.
- Service-side profitability strengthens temporarily before weakening again.
- Product-side profitability declines and then stabilizes within a new range.

Risk Concerns:
- If product GPM remains close to 60% long-term, the buffer space for profit against cost fluctuations decreases.
- If service GPM continues to fall below 50%, the overall business portfolio margin may be significantly dragged down.
