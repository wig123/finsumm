[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: A small number of operators have established a clear leading position across multiple network experience metrics.

Business Implications:
- Overall competition shows concentration at the top, with many operators possessing advantages only in specific areas.
- Operators listed in multiple categories possess greater negotiating power regarding brand value and premium pricing potential.
- High-frequency listed Nordic and Japanese operators demonstrate consistent performance across metrics.

Risk Focus:
- If Global Winners are concentrated among 3 or fewer operators, the performance gap for other operators is easily amplified.
- If an operator is listed in only 1 metric, a decline in performance in that category will result in the loss of all exposure.
