[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Gross margin experienced a significant step-down before recovering to a medium-high level.

Business Implications:
- Strong profit margin capacity existed in the early period, which was subsequently compressed temporarily.
- The middle segment showed high earnings elasticity, making it more sensitive to costs and pricing.
- In recent years, the earnings structure has been relatively contained, making volatility easier to manage.

Risk Focus:
- If the margin approaches 20% again, the profit buffer will weaken, putting pressure on short-term earnings.
- If the margin falls below 25% and persists for several years, adjustments to the product structure and cost strategy may be required.
