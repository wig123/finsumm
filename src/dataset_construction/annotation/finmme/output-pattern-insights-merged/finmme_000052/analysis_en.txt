[Pattern Characteristics]
The series evolves from an early stack with a relatively balanced contribution from both sources to a structure dominated by the dark-colored component. A trough value near zero appeared around 2012, followed by an extreme spike in 2021; subsequently, it enters a relatively stable plateau, with the light-colored component close to the baseline.

[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The source of growth shifts from Natural Growth to Net Migration dominance
