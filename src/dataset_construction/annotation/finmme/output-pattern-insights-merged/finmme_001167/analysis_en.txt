[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The composition of car production is concentrating towards medium and high-level automation.

Business Implications:
- The overall existing product portfolio is migrating towards higher levels of automation.
- Mid-level automation vehicle models will determine the primary capacity layout.
- High-level vehicle models are beginning to form an independent niche market space.

Risk Focus:
- If the proportion of L0 remains above 40% for an extended period, the pace of product renewal may be slow.
- If the proportion of L3/L4/L5 is below 3% in 2030, high-end R&D investment may struggle to achieve cost amortization.
