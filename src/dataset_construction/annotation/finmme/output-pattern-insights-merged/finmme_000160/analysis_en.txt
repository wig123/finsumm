[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The share structure is dominated by Winter and Spring; 2020 is considered a one-off anomaly.

Business Implications:

- Autumn and Summer show high sensitivity, leading to unstable forecasts
- Normal state assessment should reference 2021—2023

Risk Focus:
- Winter <70% → Decline in Winter/Spring dominance
- Any season >80% → Easy to overestimate the normal state, leading to forecast deviation
