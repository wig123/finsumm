[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The EUR interest rate swap spread between the short end and the long end has significantly narrowed after a period of high-level expansion.

Business Implications:
- The pricing differential in the term structure has significantly weakened compared to earlier periods.
- The effectiveness of swap hedging between different maturities on the curve is more aligned.
- The impact of extreme events on swap spreads has limited persistence.

Risk Focus:
- If the 2Y or 10Y spread approaches 80bp again, it could lead to a sharp increase in hedging costs.
- If the spread falls below 0bp and persists, it could lead to abnormal pricing of related interest rate positions.
