[Pattern Characteristics]
The multiple curves generally exhibit a step-wise upward shifting structure, with the overall level of subsequent years' series progressively increasing. The 2021 and 2022 series were relatively flat in the early stages, followed by a period of significant uplift before leveling off and slightly declining. The 2023 series peaked locally in the middle segment before continuing to trend lower, while 2024 and 2025, after a brief sharp jump, maintained small fluctuations within a higher range.

[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The EPS level rises year-over-year, with newer years maintaining a higher range.

Business Implications:
- Overall profitability shows a step-wise enhancement across different years.
- The later profitability benchmark is elevated, having a greater impact on valuation and pricing.
- The subsequent decline in 2023 reflects lower earnings stability compared to 2024–2025.

Risk Concerns:
- If EPS falls back below 10, it will significantly weaken market expectations formed by previous high profitability.
- If 2024/2025 EPS declines to below 13, it will erase the incremental growth achieved in the last two years.
