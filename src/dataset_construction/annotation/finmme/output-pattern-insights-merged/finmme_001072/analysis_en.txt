[Pattern Characteristics]
Overall, the total volume of the two bars remains the same across the two periods, but the internal proportional structure has undergone significant redistribution. A structural change of mutual gain and loss occurred between the mid-range segments (0.5mn-3.5mn, 3.5mn-7.5mn). The proportion of the high-end segment (7.5mn+) has clearly risen, while the proportion of the lowest segment (<0.5mn) remains minimal and stable.

[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The loan origination value structure is concentrating towards higher value segments.

Business Implications:
- Small and medium-value business has relatively contracted, and the weighting of high-value business has increased.
- The asset portfolio's reliance on high-value clients has strengthened.
- The client base and the face value level show an upward trend.

Risk Concerns:
- If the proportion of 7.5mn+ continues to exceed 30%, volatility from large single clients will amplify portfolio volatility.
- If 0.5mn-3.5mn drops below 35%, the source of business will become overly dependent on a limited high-value client base.
