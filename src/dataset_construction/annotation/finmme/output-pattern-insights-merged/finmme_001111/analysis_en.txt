Overall, the annual total showed a clear low point in 2020, having changed slowly before that, followed by a period of rapid increase, a slight pullback in the mid-to-late period, and then reaching a new high. Structurally, 407 ETR accounts for the largest proportion in all years, with NTE and other road sections stacked sequentially above it. The color blocks representing new projects gradually thicken in the later period, making the composition of the bars in the mid-to-late period more diversified. After 2026E, the relative height changes of the components are not entirely synchronized; intermediate layers slightly contract in individual years. There is no extreme situation where a single component completely dominates in any given year.

Core Conclusion: Overall toll asset revenue is expected to continue expanding, and the structure of revenue sources tends toward diversification.

- Multiple distribution points for revenue sources help reduce dependence on a single road section.

Risk Concerns:
