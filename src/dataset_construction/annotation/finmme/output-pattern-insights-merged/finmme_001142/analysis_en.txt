[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The value structure of new originations is concentrating towards mid-to-high ticket sizes.

Business Implications:
- The proportion of low-to-mid tiers is weakening, raising the monetary threshold for client acquisition.
- The expansion of mid-to-high tiers increases reliance on single-transaction output.
- Structure is concentrating towards two tiers, reducing portfolio diversity.

Risk Focus:
- If the proportion of 7.5mn+ consistently exceeds 30%, volatility from a single large-ticket client may amplify performance fluctuations.
- If 0.5–3.5mn drops below 35%, the foundation of the mid-range client base weakens, reducing resilience against volatility.
