[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: PHEV proportion maintains the lead, but BEV proportion is gradually catching up.

Business Implications:
- The importance of electrified vehicles in the structure is increasing.
- PHEV's dominance over the overall sales structure remains evident.
- The gap between BEV and PHEV proportions is narrowing, leading to a more balanced structure.

Risk Concerns:
- If the BEV proportion remains below 15% for an extended period, it may weaken the effectiveness of the pure electric product strategy.
- If the PHEV proportion surges back up towards 30%, it might suppress the potential for further structural improvement in BEV.
