[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Gasoline spreads returned to a neutral range after experiencing an extreme surge and maintained a volatile state.

Business Implications:
- The pricing environment was previously extremely tight, later transitioning to normal volatility.
- Refinery and trading profit margins were first significantly amplified, then normalized.
- Hedging and inventory strategies need to shift from extreme market mode to range-bound strategies.
