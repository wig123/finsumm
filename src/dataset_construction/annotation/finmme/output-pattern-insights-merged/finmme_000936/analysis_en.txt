[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: While the user base expands, concentration is gradually converging toward 50.4%.

Business Implications:
- Head position (market leadership) has slightly contracted relative to previous periods.
- The contribution effect of user growth on overall market share is weakening.
- Future changes in market share will be more sensitive to the competitive landscape.

Risk Focus:
