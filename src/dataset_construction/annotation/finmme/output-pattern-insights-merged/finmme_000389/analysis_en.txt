[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The degree to which the share price tracks DRAM price performance is limited, and the timing is inconsistent.

Business Implications:
- Share price valuation logic does not rely entirely on the price of a single product.
- Factors beyond earnings expectations carry significant weight in the share price.
- Short-term product price signals offer limited guidance for share price analysis.

Risk Focus:
