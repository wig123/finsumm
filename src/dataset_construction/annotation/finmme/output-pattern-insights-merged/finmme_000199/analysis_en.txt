[Chart Components]
- Chart Type: Horizontal scatter plot (with error bars), displaying three columns of values side-by-side on the right.
- Title: 5G Performance and Availability Among Nordics Top Providers; Subtitle: Speedtest Intelligence | Q2 2022.
- X-axis: Median Speeds (Mbps), scale approximately 0–500 (increments of 100); error bars indicate the 95% confidence interval.
- Y-axis: Multiple Nordic operators (listed by row, including country name).
- Right-side data columns: Download (Mbps), Upload (Mbps), 5G Availability (percentage), displayed in different colors.

[Pattern Characteristics]
- Download values are widely dispersed across the X-axis, forming a distinct dense cluster in the middle segment, while a few high-value points are separated from the main group.
- Error bars are generally short, but there are individual samples in the middle segment with significantly wider confidence intervals.
- Among the three columns on the right, the Upload distribution is more convergent, whereas the ranking of 5G Availability does not align with the Download ranking, showing a staggered distribution.

[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: A trade-off characteristic exists between high speed and coverage.

Business Implications:
- Difficulty in simultaneously achieving both speed and coverage
- Limited differentiation among mid-range providers
- Low differentiation in upload metrics

Risk Focus:
- Availability ≤ 5%, narrow coverage, limited scale
- Download < 200 Mbps, insufficient capacity for high-traffic scenarios
