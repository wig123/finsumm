[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Overall profitability remains within a stable range, with the structural focus shifting from Retail to Food.

Business Implications:
- Overall profit has not expanded significantly; it is primarily a structural adjustment.
- The Food segment's contribution to group profit is rapidly increasing.
- The Retail segment's dominant role in overall performance has significantly weakened.

Risk Focus:
- If Retail remains below 50 Wbn long-term, the segment's resilience is weak, potentially dragging down overall performance.
- If Food exceeds 250 Wbn and its proportion is too high, it indicates excessive reliance on a single segment, amplifying group-wide performance volatility.
