[Pattern Characteristics]
Overall, both curves have generally moved upward together since 2010. The total debt curve is more continuous and smooth, while the statutory limit moves upward in a step-like fashion. Near the gray vertical band around 2020, the distance between the two curves noticeably narrowed. Subsequently, total debt showed a relatively steep ascent around 2021, gradually approaching the limit while the limit remained level. By the end of the period, the total debt curve is in the highest range of the entire segment, leaving a limited gap between itself and the limit, which has remained unchanged for a long time.
