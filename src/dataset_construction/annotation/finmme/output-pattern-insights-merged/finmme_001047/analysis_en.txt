[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: In the case of higher forward rates, realised skew is relatively more dependent on the level of implied skew.

Business Implications:
- The indicative nature of the implied metric on future realised outcomes is more prominent under higher forward conditions.

- Samples with lower forward rates have a relatively limited contribution to the overall relationship.

Risk Focus:
