Core Conclusion: Short-term delinquency levels are generally converging, and structural differences are diminishing.

Business Implications:
- Default performance across categories is converging, leading to a more concentrated distribution.
- The gap between Auto loans and other categories is narrowing, resulting in a more balanced portfolio structure.
