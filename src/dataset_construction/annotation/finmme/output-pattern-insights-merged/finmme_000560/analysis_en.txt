[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The importance of WHRS (Waste Heat Recovery System) and Solar in the power generation structure is rapidly increasing and significantly amplifying during the forecast period.

Business Implications:
- Installed capacity expansion is faster than the early pace, enhancing self-sufficiency in power generation.
- Dependency on WHRS and Solar within the power source structure is significantly strengthened.
- Relative reliance on traditional power sources further declines by the end of the planning period.

Risk Concerns:
- If the installed capacity in FY26F is below 600MW, the magnitude of the power structure adjustment will be difficult to achieve the current planning target.
- If the FY26F proportion is below 50%, power generation costs and related emission reduction targets may be difficult to achieve simultaneously.
