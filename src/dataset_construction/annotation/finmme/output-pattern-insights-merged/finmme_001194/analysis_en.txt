[Pattern Characteristics]
The bar lengths for US indicators are generally concentrated near zero, with a relatively balanced distribution above and below, and only a few individual items slightly prominent in the positive and negative directions. There is no extreme divergence between US output and price indicators, and the overall spread is limited. The differences between Eurozone indicators are more pronounced, with a few items located at the sample extremes. Significant "long bars" appear on both the positive and negative sides, indicating a highly unbalanced structural characteristic.
