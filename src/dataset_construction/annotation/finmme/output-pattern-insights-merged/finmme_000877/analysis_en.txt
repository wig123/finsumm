[Pattern Characteristics]
The two lines generally exhibit a time series pattern of slight initial fluctuation, followed by a significant rise, and then a slight decline. Portable pricing consistently remains above Desktop pricing. The gap between the two is generally stable within a similar range, only slightly narrowing in the middle-to-later period before widening slightly again. The two lines formed common high points around 2021-2022, and their subsequent trends have been largely synchronized.

[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The ASPs of both PC categories, after experiencing a surge and subsequent retreat, remain in a relatively high range.

Business Implications:
- The overall profitable ASP has significantly risen compared to earlier periods.
- The premium of Portable over Desktop has maintained a stable structure long-term.
- Recent price softening indicates increased room for negotiation and configuration flexibility.

Risk Concerns:
- If Desktop ASP falls below $650, overall gross margin may be significantly compressed.
- If Portable ASP falls below $820, the narrowing price gap with Desktop may weaken the contribution of high-end products.
