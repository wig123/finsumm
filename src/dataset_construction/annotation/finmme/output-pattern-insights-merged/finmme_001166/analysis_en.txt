[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The cost level of fossil fuel units is generally higher than that of current and future wind and solar projects.

Business Implications:
- Under current gas and carbon prices, most gas and coal power generation lacks cost competitiveness.
- The reliance of wind and solar projects on the overall cost structure is more concentrated on the capital expenditure side.
- Future wind and solar projects demonstrate stronger cost advantages and greater attractiveness for deployment.

Risk Focus:
- When LCOE exceeds the Germany 1yr fwd power price of 70, project profitability margins are squeezed.
- If LCOE approaches or exceeds the Offshore wind US 2023 value of 160, investments may face significant risks of postponement or cancellation.
