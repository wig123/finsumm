[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Model iteration leads to an extreme leap in training investment and a range of uncertainty.

Business Implications:
- The driving effect of model upgrades on capital requirements is significantly enhanced.
- The cost structure is highly concentrated in the latest generation model.
- Budget preparation requires a focused assessment of the investment elasticity for future single-generation models.

Risk Focus:
- If the cost of a single model approaches or exceeds $2,000,000,000, it may breach existing annual budget ceilings.
