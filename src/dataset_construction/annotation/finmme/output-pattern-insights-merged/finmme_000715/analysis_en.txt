[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Long-term planning targets are significantly higher than the expectations of most reference scenarios.

Business Implications:
- Existing reference pathways are unlikely to naturally achieve the planned level.
- Mid-to-long-term capacity allocation and investment pace need significant acceleration.
- Differences between scenarios are widening, making decisions more sensitive to scenario selection.

Risk Focus:
- If the 2030 value falls below the labeled 2,420, achieving the high-end scenarios by 2050 will face pressure from accelerated work and increased costs.
- If 2050 only falls within the 1,500–3,000 range, a significant gap may form relative to the NZE target of approximately 7,500, impacting the realization of the overall objective.
