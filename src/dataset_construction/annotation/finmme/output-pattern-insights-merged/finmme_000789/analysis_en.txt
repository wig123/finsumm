[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Profitability performance exhibits limited correlation with tariff adjustments, showing periodic deviations.

Business Implications:
- The increase in unit price did not simultaneously lead to a proportional improvement in profitability.
- Profitability is highly dependent on other operational factors.
- The recovery in profitability during late FY23 showed a delayed reaction to the price signal.

Risk Focus:
- If the tariff remains consistently above 1.7 while EBITDA stays below 3.0, overall yield may be compressed.
- If the tariff falls below 1.4 and EBITDA is not maintained above 3.5, cash generation capability may weaken.
