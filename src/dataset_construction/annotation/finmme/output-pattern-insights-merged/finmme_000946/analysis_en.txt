[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Growth is shifting from multi-driver momentum to a moderate structure more reliant on consumption and inventories.

Business Implications:
- The current structure indicates that domestic demand is more decisive in overall performance.
- The drag on overall performance from external demand and real estate has significantly diminished.
- Overall performance during the forecast period relies more on consumption resilience than on surging investment.

Risk Focus:
- If the PCE contribution falls below 0 pp, overall growth may approach stagnation.
- If the inventory contribution falls below -1 pp again, it could trigger a renewed weakening of single-quarter overall performance.
