[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The transport sector is dominated by road travel, and emission reduction targets are focused on compressing existing high-proportion segments.

Business Implications:
- The current structure reflects a high dependency on road scenarios.
- The emission reduction pathway will primarily impact Cars and Heavy vehicles.
- Whether the 2030E target is achieved will directly determine the overall pace of emission reduction.

Risk Concerns:
- If 2030E fails to achieve the 16% reduction compared to 2019, it will undermine the credibility of the established emission reduction commitment.
- If the proportion of Cars remains high over the long term, even if the total volume approaches 600–800 million tonnes, it may constrain the optimization space for other modes.
