[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Market capitalization exhibits a significantly sensitive but not entirely symmetrical reaction to changes in profitability.

Business Implications:
- There is a strong correlation between valuation and earnings performance.
- The impact of earnings decline on valuation is smaller than the magnitude of the subsequent rise during earnings recovery.
- The terminal level indicates that both valuation and earnings are in a relatively high phase.

Risk Concerns:
- If quarterly earnings fall below 0 TWD mn again, market capitalization may repeat the significant pullback seen around 2020.
- When market capitalization exceeds 4,500 USD mn and earnings are below 1,000 TWD mn, there may be pressure due to decreased alignment between valuation and profitability.
