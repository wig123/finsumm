- The distance between the maximum and minimum segments converged, the overall structure is continuous, with no sudden jumps.

[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The loan amount structure leans more towards large ticket sizes, increasing portfolio dependency.

Business Implications:
- Reflects increased portfolio concentration.
- Increased impact on pricing and credit granting strategies.
- Weakening coverage capability of the mid-range tier.
