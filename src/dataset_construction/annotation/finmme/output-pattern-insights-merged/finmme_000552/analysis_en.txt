[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Stock price performance is chronically disconnected from analyst target expectations.

Business Implications:
- Market pricing is significantly below most target levels.
- Sell-side pricing models are slow to adjust.
- Investor adoption of research opinions is limited.

Risk Focus:
- If the stock price consistently remains below USD 120, it may weaken the effectiveness of subsequent financing and equity incentive plans.
- If the target price consistently exceeds the stock price by more than USD 50, it may lead to overly optimistic investment decisions.
