[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Market size is expanding rapidly, the structure is dominated by hardware, and the short-term landscape is stable.

Business Implications:
- Expanding expenditure volume indicates a sustained increase in willingness to invest.
- Hardware-dominated structure, capital is primarily concentrated on foundational capability construction.
- Growth rate remains in the moderate range, which is favorable for medium-term planning and budget preparation.

Risk Concerns:
- If YoY growth falls below 10%, it could lead to a slowdown in new project initiation and expansion.
- If the hardware proportion remains above 70% long-term, it may crowd out investment in software and service capabilities.
