[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Electrification is dominant, but the 2030 compliance buffer is extremely narrow.

Business Implications:
- Compliance level primarily depends on electrification penetration.
- High sensitivity to the achievement of emission control targets.
- The marginal contribution of traditional powertrains significantly weakens.

Risk Focus:
- If 2030 Actual > 42 g/km, a positive difference will occur, failing to meet the Cap.
- If 2030 EV+FCV < 7.2 mn, the difficulty of meeting the standard increases, leading to insufficient buffer.
