[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The overall FCF plan targets a higher long-term level compared to the FY21 normalised value.

Business Implications:
- Most business lines are expected to contribute positively to future cash flow.
- Individual segments may potentially drag down overall Free Cash Flow performance.
- Japan Life is relatively sensitive to accounting standards, affecting the scale of distributable cash.

Risk Focus:
- If actual FCF falls below the FY21 normalised value of 1.2, the achievement of medium-to-long-term targets will be hindered.
- If any Segment shifts from an upward to a downward trend, the attainability of the "mid single-digit" range will be weakened.
