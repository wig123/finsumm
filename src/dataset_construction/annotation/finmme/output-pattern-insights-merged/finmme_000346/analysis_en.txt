[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The revenue structure is highly dependent on Replacement, with OEM and Export serving as secondary supplements.

Business Implications:
- Revenue sources are concentrated in a single segment, leading to limited resilience against volatility.
- The increase in OEM proportion indicates a strengthening contribution from original equipment supply.
- The volume of Export is limited, resulting in minimal impact on the overall structure.

Risk Focus:
- If the Replacement proportion consistently exceeds 60%, a decline in this segment will amplify profit volatility.
- If Export remains below 10% for the long term, it will limit the ability of external markets to diversify overall demand.
