[Pattern Characteristics]
The structure in each period consists of a single large block and several small blocks, with limited overall structural change between different fiscal years and quarters. Truck & Bus consistently accounts for more than half, followed by Pass car. The remaining three categories have smaller proportions and their combined total remains within a narrow range. Agri showed a relative high point in FY22. Others maintained a higher level after hitting a low point between FY21 and FY22, while LCV remained largely stable across all periods.

[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: The structure is consistently dominated by Truck & Bus, with an increasing proportion of Passenger Cars (Pass car).

Business Implications:
- Revenue sources are highly concentrated in Truck & Bus.
- Increased weighting of Pass car, indicating a slight shift towards structural diversification.
- Limited change in minor categories, resulting in limited impact on the overall structure.

Risk Focus:
- If the proportion of Truck & Bus remains consistently above 55% and weakens, overall volatility will be amplified.
- When the combined total of Others and Agri falls below 10%, diversification buffer capacity significantly decreases.
