[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: Leverage significantly amplifies the profitability level and the sensitivity to price parameters.

Business Implications:
- IRR is primarily driven by PPA price, and secondarily by turbine unit price (ASP).
- Applying leverage significantly increases return elasticity, and the range of fluctuation is magnified.
- Under a combination of medium-to-high PPA and medium ASP, leverage can significantly elevate the project return range.

Risk Focus:
- When PPA ≤ $20 and ASP ≥ $1.10, the Levered IRR approaches or falls below 0%, potentially leading to negative equity returns.
- Within the PPA ≤ $35 range, every $0.05/W increase in ASP significantly compresses the Levered IRR, potentially causing marginal projects to quickly fall outside the target return.
