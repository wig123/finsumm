[Core Insights]
Core Conclusion: ROCE is at a high level, indicating outstanding capital efficiency performance.

Business Implications:
- Reflects advantages in profitability and capital utilization efficiency.
- Forms a positive signal for capital allocation and expansion decisions.
- Strengthens shareholder return expectations and capital attractiveness.

Risk Focus:
- ROCE < 35%: Insufficient return on investment, restricted reinvestment.
- ROCE ≤ 24%: Returning to 2020 levels, expansion plans would be hindered.
